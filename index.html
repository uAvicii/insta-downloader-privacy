Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What data we collect

This extension collects no personal data. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any information about you or your activity.

How the extension works

The extension runs entirely in your browser. It reads the Instagram profile page you are currently viewing and uses your existing browser login session to fetch and download media files directly to your device. No data is sent to any external server operated by us.

Permissions used

  • tabs – to read the current tab URL for auto-filling the username field.
  • scripting – to execute fetch requests in the Instagram tab using your existing session.
  • downloads – to save media files to your local device.
  • storage – to save download progress state locally in the browser.

Third-party services

This extension communicates only with www.instagram.com using your own authenticated session. No third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.
